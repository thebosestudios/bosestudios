About Us

Who We Are

Bose Studios is the game development arm of Bose Ventures LLC. We design, develop, and publish mobile games on the Google Play Store. Our mission is to create entertaining, high-quality gaming experiences that players enjoy coming back to.

Whether it's a quick puzzle break or an immersive adventure, we focus on polished gameplay, clean design, and respect for our players' time and privacy.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

Bose Ventures LLC ("Bose Studios," "we," "us," or "our") operates mobile applications published under the developer name Bose Studios on the Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our apps.

1. Information We Collect

Our apps may collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and general diagnostic data to help us fix bugs and improve performance.
  • Usage Data: Gameplay analytics such as levels completed, session duration, and in-app actions to help us improve the gaming experience.
  • Advertising Data: If our apps display ads, our third-party ad partners may collect advertising identifiers and general interaction data. Please refer to the respective ad network's privacy policy for more details.

We do not knowingly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or precise location data unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., when contacting us for support).

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to operate and improve our apps, fix technical issues and bugs, analyze usage trends and gameplay patterns, and serve relevant advertisements (if applicable).

3. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services that collect information. These may include Google Play Services, Google AdMob or other ad networks, and Firebase Analytics. Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review those policies.

4. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through our apps. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. You can also uninstall our apps at any time to stop all data collection associated with that app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us
